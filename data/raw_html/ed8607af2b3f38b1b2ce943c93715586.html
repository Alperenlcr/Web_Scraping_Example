Federal President condoles the President of Sri Lanka
April 21, 2019

Federal President Frank-Walter Steinmeier has condoled the President of Sri Lanka, Maithripala Sirisena. The Federal President writes:

"Disgusted and horrified, I follow the terrible news about the cowardly terrorist attacks in Sri Lanka, in which so many innocent people were killed and many more were injured. It is particularly vile that numerous peaceful worshipers in churches on Easter Sunday became the target of these sneaky attacks.
Germany stands by your side in the determination to confront inhuman terror. I convey to you, also on behalf of my compatriots, my heartfelt sympathy at this difficult hour. I wish the injured people strength and speedy recovery. My thoughts are also with the bereaved families and friends of the victims and the helpers on site. "
